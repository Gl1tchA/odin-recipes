Lasagna Recipe

Originating from the homes of Italian cuisine along with it's pasta cousins, Lasagna offers a unique taste to what you can do with dough, tomato sauce and a bit of cheese!

Even though it looks quite intimidating to make, with modern advancement to our food industry, we now no longer have to knead our own doughs in order to create the iconic square pasta the lasagna has, along with tomato sauce, cheese and a bit of meat, all that is left is to pop them in any type of oven and wait for the magic to happen. Easier said than done right? So let me teach you how to cook this dish step by step so you can taste the magic yourself!

Ingredients

  • Square Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Pepper, salt and preferred garnishes

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauace, boil some water in a pan, add the tomato sauce, the meat you bough and garnishes like pepper, onion or what ever it is that you made!
  2. While the sauce simmers, boil the lasagna pasta until it is soft and noodly. Add salt,pepper or other spices to give tatse with every bite
  3. After the sauce and the pasta is prepared, assemble the lasagna according to your wishes, either by pasta/sauce/cheese/pasta/sauce/cheese/... or if you are mentally insane, pasta/pasta/pasta/sauce/cheese. It's your choice
  4. Once you are done assembling the meat monster, pop it into the oven and play the waiting game.
